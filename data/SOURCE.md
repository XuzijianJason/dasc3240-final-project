# Dataset Source

Dataset: Teen Social Media Usage & Mental Health

Source URL: https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/sureshbeekhani/teen-social-media-usage-and-mental-health/data

Creator: SURESH BEEKHANI

Kaggle subtitle: "Analyzing how social media affects teen stress, sleep, and anxiety"

License: MIT

Last updated on Kaggle: 2026-04-24

Accessed for this project: 2026-04-25

Notes:

- The dataset contains 2,500 synthetic teen-level records about daily social media use, platform usage, sleep, bedtime screen time, academic performance, physical activity, social interaction, stress, anxiety, and depression risk.
- The Kaggle page states that the records are synthetic and generated for educational and research purposes. They do not represent real individuals.
- The Kaggle page also describes built-in relationship logic, including higher social media usage increasing stress and anxiety, lower sleep increasing stress and depression risk, and more physical activity reducing stress and anxiety.
- The app should present findings as simulation-based patterns, not as real-world causal evidence.
